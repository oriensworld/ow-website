## Overview

Higanbana is a mix-media image creation produced in Taitung City, Taiwan in 2020. The red line symbolizes longing for a person, with images representing thoughts directed at that person, reassembled and entwined by the red line, expressing emotional release.

## Features

- Mix-media composition — photographs combined with physical red thread to create layered visual narratives
- Emotional symbolism — the red line represents longing and connection, binding fragmented images into a cohesive expression
- 15-piece series — a grid of individual compositions that form a larger collective work
