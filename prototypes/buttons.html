How to see the difference

Look at the corners of the cards and buttons. With border-radius, the straight edge abruptly transitions into a circular arc. With superellipse, the edge gradually eases into the curve — the corner "hugs" the straight edge longer before rounding. The difference is most visible on the 80×80 card and the large button.
